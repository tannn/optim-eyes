OptimEyes your eye health

Many people who use the computer daily have to deal with Computer Vision Syndrome. To help give your eyes a break, we suggest following the 20-20-20 rule. Every 20 minutes, take a break to look at something 20 feet away for 20 seconds. We'll remind you every 20 minutes to take a break.

We'll also warn you if you're too close to the screen. This causes unneeded eyestrain and poor posture. This is reminders are all automatic and runs in the background, so you can focus on your work.
